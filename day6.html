A paragraph is a self-contained unit of writing that typically focuses on a single idea or point. It usually consists of several sentences, often between five and ten, and is visually distinguished by an indentation at the beginning of the first line or a line break. Paragraphs help organize written work and make it easier for readers to follow the author's train of thought.
